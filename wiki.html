Introduction

Mob Trophies is a comprehensive Hytale mod that adds collectible trophies for every creature in the game. Each trophy represents a milestone in your adventure, allowing you to showcase your victories in your base.

The mod revolves around a progression system based on the Trophy Workbench, where you collect fragments and heads to craft high-quality decorations.

Trophy Workbench

The Workbench is the heart of the mod. It is not just a crafting table, but an evolving tool that requires upgrades to unlock more complex trophies.

  • Tier 1: Basic wooden trophies and common mob heads.
  • Advanced Tiers: Unlock glow effects, animated trophies, and rare boss statues.

Mob Drops & Trophies

Creatures don't just drop trophies; they drop Fragments and Heads. You need to combine these at the workbench.

Drop rates vary depending on the rarity of the mob. Some trophies can only be obtained in specific biomes or during special events.

Recycling System

Got too many duplicate fragments? The recycling system allows you to break down unwanted trophy parts into raw materials or Trophy Essence, used for high-tier upgrades.

Statue Effects

Statues are the ultimate form of trophies. Once placed, they can be customized with special effects:

  • Glow: Makes parts of the statue illuminate in the dark.
  • Particles: Adds ambient effects like smoke, fire.

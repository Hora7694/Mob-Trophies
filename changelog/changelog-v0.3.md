* **Introduction of a dedicated workbench**: A new workbench is now craftable with [...]. It allows you to craft wall-mounted heads, obtain statue variants, and use the 'Fragments' trophies introduced in the last update to transform them into decorative objects/items or recycle them.

* **Adding variants for statues**: Each statue now has at least 1 variant related to the material of where it lives (below is a list of added statues) obtainable in the new 'Trophy Workbench'.

* **Addition of 'Mythic' rarity**: The 'Mythic' rarity has been added; it is located between 'Epic' and 'Legendary' and only concerns statues.
